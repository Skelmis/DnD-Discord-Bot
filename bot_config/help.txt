__**Current Commands**__

:gear: ``help``
- Shows this help message.

:gear: ``stats``
- Shows some bot stats

:gear: ``echo``
- Repeats your message
- echo (message)

:gear: ``purge``
- Purge chats
- purge (number of messages)
- *Requires role: `Config`*

:gear: ``modules``
- List all cogs

:gear: ``perms``
- List a users permissions
- perms <user>

:gear: ``roll``
- Roll the dice
- roll (times) (dice sides) <multiplier/advantage/disadvantage>
- E.g. !roll 10d20 is now !roll 10 20

:gear: ``setskill``
- Set values for your skills
- setskill (skill name) (value/changes) (type)

:gear: ``skills``
- Shows all your stored skills

:gear: ``embed``
- Embed your own custom message.
- embed (message)
